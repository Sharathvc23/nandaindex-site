Status

The NandaIndex Federated Resolution Architecture described on this site is a working draft. Its entries are AI Catalog Catalog Entries. Its value is in making existing discovery systems reachable from one another, and in staying out of the critical path where direct resolution already works.

The example identities used across this site (such as example.com, moonbakery.com, and john@hotmail.com) are illustrative. They do not refer to real organisations, services, or individuals.
